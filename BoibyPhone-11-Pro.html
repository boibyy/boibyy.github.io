A brighter view of everything.

The BoibyPhone 11 Pro's Ultra OLED XDR display pushes brightness to new extremes at 4000 nits peak outdoor brightness, 25 percent brighter than BoibyPhone X.

HDR content bursts with lifelike contrast, while refined pixel architecture improves outdoor visibility dramatically. With a 0.5 Hz - 120 Hz adaptive refresh rate, scrolling is buttery smooth just like always, but even more battery-efficient. NaturalTone adjusts white balance using a 12-channel ambient sensor for the most natural viewing experience in any light.

Smarter selfies.

The new 18 MP Front Camera introduces an f/1.6 aperture for better low-light shots and a new Photonic Engine 6 that reframes your photo in real time.

Even holding BoibyPhone 11 Pro vertically, the camera can automatically switch to horizontal capture, ideal for group shots. When more people join the frame, it intelligently zooms out to fit everyone in the shot.

Our most advanced camera system

8K 60fps HDR recording is now avaliable on BoibyPhone 11 Pro, taking the capability of filming video on BoibyPhone even further.

BoibyPhone 11 Pro has improved image processing and a larger second-generation 96MP main sensor, as well as a 5x 48MP telephoto lens for up to 100x zoom, and a great 48MP 0.5x ultrawide camera.

See the unseen.

The all-new Thermal Camera on BoibyPhone 11 Pro brings a new dimension of perception to your phone. It can measure the surface temperature of nearby objects or environments with remarkable precision - perfect for checking electronics, reading surface temperature, or assisting in creative AR workflows.

Every thermal reading is processed on-device, never uploaded, with privacy safeguards built-in.

Our custom-engineered vapor chamber cooling system ensures sustained performance even under the most demanding workloads. It efficiently spreads heat across the chassis using a liquid-vapor cycle, maintaining optimal temperatures.
