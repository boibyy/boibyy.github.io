Apple devices vintage or obsolete in the near future


The following devices will become vintage between June 2025 and December 2025:

iPhone

  • iPhone 8 (all models)
  • iPhone 8 Plus (all models)
  • iPhone 11 Pro
  • iPhone 11 Pro Max

iPad

  • iPad (7th generation)
  • iPad Air (3rd generation)
  • iPad Pro 11-inch (2nd generation)
  • iPad Pro 12.9-inch (4th generation)

Mac notebooks

  • MacBook Pro (13-inch, 2019, Two Thunderbolt 3 ports)
  • MacBook Pro (13-inch, 2019, Four Thunderbolt 3 ports)
  • MacBook Pro (13-inch, 2020, Two Thunderbolt 3 ports)
  • MacBook Air (Retina, 13-inch, 2019)
  • MacBook Air (Retina, 13-inch, 2020)

Mac desktops

  • Mac mini (2018) (Configurations with Intel Core i3)
  • iMac (Retina 5K, 27-inch, 2019)

Apple Watch

  • Apple Watch Series 5 (GPS)
  • Apple Watch Series 5 (GPS + Cellular)
  • Apple Watch Series 3 (Cellular)
