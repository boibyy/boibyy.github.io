Boiby Community Rules

All users are required to abide by these rules, as well as the Terms of Use.

For Restricted (17+) games, please see Boiby’s Restricted Content Policy.



Swearing and Profanity

Profanity is not allowed. We strive to maintain a respectful and inclusive environment for all members of the Boiby community. Please refrain from using any form of profane language, including but not limited to swear words, offensive slurs, and inappropriate expressions.

Examples of violations:

  • Using explicit swear words in posts or comments.
  • Directing offensive language at other users.

In 17+ games and chats, users are allowed to use profanity, as long as it is not offensive, excessive, or directed at others. For more information on our restricted content policies, visit Boiby Restricted Content Policy.



Threats, Bullying, and Harassment

Threats, Bullying, and harassment are strictly prohibited. The Boiby community is a safe space for everyone, and any form of bullying, intimidation, or targeted harassment will not be tolerated. This includes, but is not limited to, hate speech, threats, personal attacks, and any behavior intended to demean or harm others.

Examples of violations:

  • Sending threatening messages to another user.
  • Repeatedly targeting a user with insults or demeaning comments.
  • Encouraging others to harass or bully someone.


Discrimination, Slurs, and Hate Speech

Discrimination, slurs, and hate speech are not tolerated. The Boiby community is committed to fostering an inclusive and welcoming environment for people of all backgrounds, identities, and beliefs. Any form of discrimination, hate speech, or use of slurs—whether based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other characteristic—is strictly prohibited.

Examples of violations:

  • Using racial or ethnic slurs.
  • Making derogatory comments about someone's religion or beliefs.
  • Promoting hateful ideologies or groups.


Violent Content and Gore

Violent content and gore are not allowed. To ensure a safe and comfortable environment for all users, any content that depicts graphic violence, gore, or harm to individuals or animals is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, images, videos, or descriptions of physical abuse, self-harm, or extreme violence.

Examples of violations:

  • Sharing graphic images or videos of violence.
  • Glorifying or encouraging self-harm.
  • Posting detailed descriptions of harmful or violent acts.

In 17+ games, extreme violence and gore are allowed, as long as it is not of a real person or depicting real people, or animals. For more information on our restricted content policies, visit Boiby Restricted Content Policy.



Romantic and Sexual Content

Romantic and sexual content is not permitted. The Boiby community is intended to be a safe and respectful space for all users. Any content that is sexually explicit, suggestive, or promotes inappropriate romantic behavior is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, explicit images, videos, or text.

Examples of violations:

  • Sharing sexually explicit images or videos.
  • Posting suggestive or inappropriate comments.
  • Promoting or soliciting romantic or sexual interactions.


Illegal and Regulated Goods and Activities

Illegal and regulated goods and activities are strictly prohibited. The Boiby community does not allow any content that promotes, facilitates, or encourages illegal activities or the sale of regulated goods.

Examples of violations:

  • Selling or promoting illegal drugs.
  • Advertising the sale of weapons or counterfeit goods.
  • Encouraging illegal activities, such as hacking or fraud.

In 17+ games, depictions of alcohol are allowed, though any encouragement of real-life alcohol consumption or use is not. For more information on our restricted content policies, visit Boiby Restricted Content Policy.



Political Content

Political content is not allowed. To maintain a neutral and inclusive environment, any content related to politics, political figures, or political movements is strictly prohibited.

Examples of violations:

  • Posting about political parties or candidates.
  • Sharing political opinions or propaganda.
  • Discussing political events or controversies.


Scams

Scams are strictly prohibited. The Boiby community is committed to maintaining a trustworthy environment. Any form of scam or fraudulent activity, including phishing attempts, fake giveaways, or financial fraud, is not allowed.

Examples of violations:

  • Posting fake giveaways or contests.
  • Sending phishing links or scams.
  • Attempting to defraud other users.


Spam

Spam is not allowed. The Boiby community values meaningful and relevant interactions. Any form of repetitive, irrelevant, or unsolicited content is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, excessive posting, chain messages, or irrelevant links.

Examples of violations:

  • Posting repetitive or irrelevant content.
  • Sending unsolicited messages or advertisements.
  • Sharing chain messages or unrelated links.


Intellectual Property Violations

Intellectual property violations are strictly prohibited. The Boiby community respects the rights of content creators and prohibits the unauthorized use, distribution, or reproduction of copyrighted material. This includes, but is not limited to, images, videos, music, and written content. Violations of this rule will result in immediate content removal and may lead to account suspension or permanent bans.

Examples of violations:

  • Sharing copyrighted material without permission.
  • Claiming someone else's work as your own.


Sharing Personal Information

Sharing personal information is not allowed. The Boiby community prioritizes the privacy and safety of its users. Any content that reveals personal information about yourself or others—such as addresses, phone numbers, or private messages—is strictly prohibited.

Examples of violations:

  • Posting someone's phone number or address without consent.
  • Sharing private messages or emails publicly.
  • Revealing sensitive personal information about others.


Misusing Boiby Systems

Any attempt to exploit, bypass, or manipulate Boiby's safety systems, moderation tools, or account features is not allowed. This includes actions that undermine the integrity of the platform or evade enforcement measures.

Examples of violations:

  • Attempting to get prohibited content around our safety systems, including by hiding violative elements of assets.
  • Creating or using an alternate account to avoid moderation actions taken against another account.
  • Offering access to your account or soliciting access to another’s account for the purpose of evading enforcement action or safety systems.


Disruptive Behavior or Content

Disruptive behavior or content is not allowed. The Boiby community values a positive and constructive environment. Any actions or content that disrupt the experience of others, interfere with platform functionality, or violate community trust are strictly prohibited.

Examples of violations:

  • Falsely reporting other users or experiences or encouraging others to do so.
  • Using bots that are programmed to run disruptive tasks.
  • Uploading excessively loud or distorted audio.


Boiby Endangerment

Endangering Boiby is strictly prohibited. Any threats, harmful actions, or content that jeopardizes the safety, security, and wellbeing of Boiby (the cat) is not allowed. Violations of this rule will result in an immediate permanent ban.

Examples of violations:

  • Making threats to harm Boiby.
  • Encouraging or participating in activities that could harm Boiby.


Account Theft

Account theft or unauthorized access is strictly prohibited. The Boiby community values the security and privacy of its users. Any attempt to steal, hack, or gain unauthorized access to another user's account is not allowed.

Examples of violations:

  • Attempting to hack or steal another user's account.
  • Sharing or using someone else's login credentials without permission.
  • Soliciting or offering account access for malicious purposes.


Impersonation

Impersonation is strictly prohibited. The Boiby community values authenticity and trust. Pretending to be someone else, including other users, Boiby employees, or public figures, is not allowed. This includes using similar usernames, profile details, or content to mislead others.

Examples of violations:

  • Creating an account that mimics another user or Boiby employee.
  • Using someone else's name, image, or identity to deceive others.
  • Pretending to represent Boiby or an official Boiby partner.


Abuse of Boiby Employees

Abuse of Boiby employees is strictly prohibited. Boiby employees are here to support the community and ensure a safe environment. Any form of harassment, threats, or disrespectful behavior directed at Boiby employees will not be tolerated.

Examples of violations:

  • Sending threatening or abusive messages to Boiby employees.
  • Harassing or targeting Boiby employees.
  • Encouraging others to harm or harass Boiby employees.


Misinformation

Spreading misinformation is strictly prohibited. The Boiby community is committed to promoting accurate and trustworthy information. Any content that spreads false or misleading information, especially on topics that could harm individuals, is not allowed. This includes, but is not limited to, false advice, conspiracy theories, and fabricated information or news.

Examples of violations:

  • Sharing false or misleading information.
  • Promoting dangerous conspiracy theories or unfounded claims.
  • Spreading fabricated news or manipulated media.
