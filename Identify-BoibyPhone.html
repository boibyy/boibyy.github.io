BoibyPhone SE (3rd generation)

Year Introduced: 2025

Year Discontinued: N/A

Colours: White, Black, Blue

Capacity: 128 GB, 256 GB

Memory: 8 GB, 12 GB

BoibyPhone SE (2nd generation) has an all-screen 6.2-inch Super OLED display with a dynamic bubble at the top centre of the display. It has a flat aluminium design with a glass back. The Boiby Button and silent ringer are on the top left-hand side and the side button is on the right-hand side of the device. There are two cameras on the back, situated in the camera pill on the top left of the back of the phone. There’s an LED flash to the right of the camera housing.

Status: Hardware and software fully supported


BoibyPhone 11 Pro Max

Year Introduced: 2025

Year Discontinued: N/A

Colours: Pacific Blue, Pearl White, Light Gold, Space Black

Capacity: 256 GB, 512 GB, 1 TB, 2 TB

Memory: 16 GB, 24 GB

BoibyPhone 11 Pro Max has a 7-inch all-screen Ultra OLED Infinity display with no visible cutouts and extremely thin bezels. It has a titanium unibody design with a textured matte glass back and a two-tone design. The Boiby button and silent ringer are on the top left-hand side and the side button is on the right-hand side of the device. The Camera Control is on the bottom right-hand side. There are three cameras on the back, situated in the large pill-shaped plateau that spans the top of the phone, with a 192 MP main, 48 MP ultrawide, and 48 MP 5x Telephoto. There’s a LiDAR Scanner and an LED flash on the right-hand side of the plateau.

Status: Hardware and software fully supported
