About Boiby

What is the Boiby App?

Boiby is a tiny virtual desktop cat that lives inside a window on your computer. Built in Godot, Boiby responds to simple keyboard controls: press Space to hear a meow, and E to change Boiby's image. It's intentionally playful — part pet, part toy — designed to bring a little character to your desktop.

The project is currently in beta (version beta 3.0) and available for Windows, macOS and Linux.

Controls & Features

  • Space - play one of Boiby's meows (6 meows available in beta).
  • E - cycle Boiby's images (2 images available in beta).
  • Resizable windowed mode; toggles and preferences are planned for future releases.
More meows, images and features are planned.

Supported Platforms

Boiby beta 3.0 runs on:

  • Windows 10 (64-bit) and later
  • macOS 11 Big Sur and later
  • Modern 64-bit Linux distributions

A GPU that supports Vulkan rendering is required for the Godot renderer to function properly (see download page for details).

About the Beta

This is an experimental build. Expect occasional crashes, missing features, and rough edges. Your feedback is very welcome — if you find bugs or have suggestions, open an issue on the project's GitHub or contact me through the site.
