Upgrading the memory in your BoibyBook Pro (2024 and later)

Updated October 9, 2025

Memory Compatibility

  • BoibyBook Pro 14-inch B5 (2024) & B6 (2025): 16 GB, 24 GB, or 32 GB LPDDR5-7500 LPCAMM2 (one module supported).
  • BoibyBook Pro 14-inch & 16-inch B5 Pro (2024) & B6 Pro (2025): 32 GB, 48 GB, or 64 GB LPDDR5X-8533 LPCAMM2 (one module supported).
  • BoibyBook Pro 14-inch & 16-inch B5 Max (2024) & B6 Max (2025): 48 GB, 64 GB, 96 GB, or 128 GB LPDDR5X-8533 LPCAMM2 (two modules supported; both modules must match capacity and timing, and sockets must be fully populated, or the system may not boot correctly).

Before You Begin

  • Confirm your exact BoibyBook Pro variant and check its LPCAMM2 socket count and supported capacity.
  • Back up all data (for example, via Boiby Time or another backup solution).
  • Prepare tools: Torx (T5, T6) screwdriver, and a plastic spudger or opening pick..

Step 1: Power Off and Disconnect

Shut down the BoibyBook Pro fully, and unplug all peripherals, power cables, and external devices.

Step 2: Remove the Bottom Cover

Flip the unit over. Remove all Torx screws securing the bottom cover.

Use a spudger to gently pry up the cover, releasing clips one edge at a time. Lift off and set aside safely.

Step 3: Disconnect Battery

Lift and disconnect the battery connector from the logic board to prevent power during this process.

Step 4: Remove the LPCAMM2 Module(s)

  • Locate the LPCAMM2 modules on the logic board.
  • Loosen the three screws to lift the bracket off the module.
  • Grip the LPCAMM2 board by its edges and carefully lift it straight upward (perpendicular) from the compression connector. Do not touch the contacts.
  • If your BoibyBoook Pro uses two modules, repeat for the second socket, ensuring both modules match exactly in orientation, and are the same specification.

Step 5: Install the New LPCAMM2 Module(s)

  • Align the new LPCAMM2 module’s alignment pegs and notch with the logic board connector. The orientation must match exactly.
  • Gently press straight down until the module seats flush with the connector. Avoid tilting.
  • Place the bracket back over the module and tighten screws in a symmetrical sequence (center → left → right). Do not over-torque
  • For dual-module systems, install the second LPCAMM2 module in the same way, ensuring both modules are identical in capacity, speed, and orientation.

Step 6: Reconnect Battery & Reassemble

  • Reattach the battery connector to the logic board.

Step 7: Power On and Verify

  • Reconnect power and peripherals, then power on the BoibyBook Pro.
  • Open About This BoibyBook → Memory and confirm the full capacity and correct speed of the newly installed LPCAMM2 module(s).
  • If the system fails to boot or does not detect the memory, shut down and reseat the module(s), ensuring alignment and full contact. Mismatched modules or incorrect seating can prevent boot.

Tips & Notes

  • Always handle the LPCAMM2 by its edges; do not touch the contacts.
  • Use Boiby-certified LPCAMM2 modules matched in timing, speed, and revision to guarantee compatibility.
  • Do not force a module into place—if it does not seat cleanly, double-check orientation and alignment pegs.
  • When tightening bracket screws, do so gently but firmly; excess torque can warp the module or logic board.
