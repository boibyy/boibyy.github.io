How much RAM do I need?

16 GB: Good for everyday tasks like web browsing, office applications, general multitasking, as well as photo editing and light video editing or development.

32 GB: Recommended for power users, developers, and content creators/video editors. Good for lots of browser tabs and runbing multiple applications simultaneously.

64 GB: Ideal for 4K video editing with complex timelines, large development projects, 3D modelling/rendering, virtual machines, and data science. Note: Only available with B2 Neo.

Which chip should I pick?

Boiby B2 Plus (10 cores): Great performance for most users and great efficiency.

Boiby B2 Neo (12 cores): Ideal for those who need more performance for more demanding workflows or tasks.

Which operating system is right for me?

boibyOS 25.11: Boiby's custom operating system designed with Boiby hardware in mind. Offers optimized performance, seamless integration, and a clean interface.

Windows 11 Pro: Familiar Windows environment with full compatibility for business and productivity software, games, and specialized applications.

No operating system: For users who prefer to install their own OS (Linux distributions, etc.).
